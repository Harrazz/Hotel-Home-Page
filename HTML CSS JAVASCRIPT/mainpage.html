Attraction

Pantai Chenang

Pantai Chenang is a picturesque beach located on the western coast of Langkawi Island in Malaysia. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations on the island, known for its stunning natural beauty and vibrant atmosphere.

Cable Car

The Langkawi Cable Car, also known as the Langkawi SkyCab, is a popular tourist attraction located on the island of Langkawi in Malaysia. It is one of the steepest cable car rides in the world and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the island's lush landscapes and surrounding seas.

Dataran Lang

Dataran Lang, also known as Eagle Square, is a prominent landmark and popular tourist attraction located in the heart of Kuah, the main town of Langkawi Island in Malaysia. It is known for its iconic 12-meter-tall sculpture of an eagle poised for flight.

Jambatan Gantung

Jambatan Gantung Langkawi, also known as the Langkawi Sky Bridge, is a spectacular suspended bridge located on Langkawi Island in Malaysia. It is one of the island's most popular tourist attractions, offering breathtaking views and an exhilarating experience.

Tasik Dayang Bunting

Tasik Dayang Bunting, also known as Lake of the Pregnant Maiden, is a beautiful freshwater lake located on Dayang Bunting Island, one of the islands in the Langkawi archipelago in Malaysia. It is a popular tourist destination known for its serene surroundings, crystal-clear waters, and fascinating legends.

Kilim Geoforest Park

The Kilim Geoforest Park is a captivating nature reserve located in Langkawi, Malaysia. Nestled in the northern part of the island, this park is renowned for its stunning geological formations, diverse ecosystem, and abundant wildlife.
